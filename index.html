Who are the Moomins?

The Moomins are a creation of author Tove Jansson. They Moomin family is Moomintroll, Moominmamma, and Mooinpappa who all live in Moominvalley with other creatures and characters. Scroll below to learn about the Moomins and their freinds.

About the author

Born in Helsinki, Finland, Jansson's family were part of a Swedish-speaking minority in Finland. She wrote her first Moomin book in 1945, during World War II, called The Moomins and the Great Flood. Although gone mostly unnoticed her next two books garnered her more recognition, Comet in Moominland and Finn Family Moomintroll.

Moomin

Moomintroll or simply, Moomin, he is, well, a Moomin; a fun loving young boy from Moominvalley. He always tries to be good but sometimes his curiosity gets him into trouble. Brave and always wanting to make his friends happy, Moomin is the best friend anyone could have! Just don't call him a hippo!

MoominMamma & MoominPappa

The perfect yin and yang. Moominmpappa is a restless soul. When he was young, he left the orphanage to venture out into the world. Now that he is settled down as a father, he is often writing stories of his life to share with his family. Moominmamma is the steady hand of the family. Always calm and rarely angry. She is never without her purse. It holds handy essential items like dry socks and pudding. It might even hold hopes and dreams!

Snufkin

The wanderer of Moominvalley, Snufkin has adventure in his heart. He can be a loner but always has time for his friend Moomin. He loves fishing, playing his harmonica and travel.

Little My

She may be small but never underestimate her. This naughty little girl loves to cause trouble and longs for adventure. Watch out, she likes to bite!
