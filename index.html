Who are the Moomins?

The Moomins are a creation of author Tove Jansson. They Moomin family is Moomintroll, Moominmamma, and Mooinpappa who all live in Moominvalley with other creatures and characters. Scroll below to learn about the Moomins and their freinds.

About the author

Born in Helsinki, Finland, Jansson's family were part of a Swedish-speaking minority in Finland. She wrote her first Moomin book in 1945, during World War II, called The Moomins and the Great Flood. Although gone mostly unnoticed her next two books garnered her more recognition, Comet in Moominland and Finn Family Moomintroll.
